ABOUT

My name is Sebastiano Laini, and I´m a computer technician and a junior Front-end Web developer.

What is programming for Me?

Web programming makes easier the communication between companies and customers.
Since I was a child, I have always enjoyed everything that has something to do with technology: computers, video games…and finally, what started as a hobby became in my profession. I love it so much that in my free time I also try to do something related to technology. For me, is essential to keep myself updated and motivated.

Employment evolution and concerns

I started my work experience as a hardware technician, meeting the companies need and offering consulting by software (companies management). Continuing with my career, I specialized in Apple´s systems and devices, that gave me the opportunity to work in the Apple Store of Madrid Sol.

Interacting in that ambit I met lots of people, some of them related to the world of programming, and what began as simple curiosity became my professional life´s priority.

In January 2016 I enrolled a course in “Treehouse”, and that is how the adventure started. I chose a tech-degree as a Front Web Developer. I have made eleven projects in which I have learned the principles of programming and the good practices to start this new road that I´ve chosen.
